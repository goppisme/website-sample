Biryani recipe

Biryani is a fragrant rice dish in India, cooked with meat (chicken, lamb etc.), spices, and sometimes vegetables.Each region has its own twist, like the Hyderabadi dum biryani.Basmati rice is layered with the flavorful ingredients and often sealed for a slow cook.The result is a colorful, aromatic, and satisfying main course enjoyed for celebrations or everyday meals.
